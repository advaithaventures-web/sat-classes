Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for sessions?

After you contact Ramesh via WhatsApp, he'll send you secure payment instructions. We accept payments via bank transfer (US & India), UPI (India), PayPal, Wise, and other international payment methods. Once payment is confirmed, we'll schedule your session with Raj.

Who is Ramesh?

Ramesh is our scheduling coordinator who handles all bookings, payment coordination, and session scheduling. He'll assist you from initial inquiry through to confirming your session with Raj. For teaching-related questions, Ramesh will connect you directly with Raj.

Can I schedule sessions at different times?

Absolutely! When you purchase hourly tutoring, you can schedule sessions at times that work for your schedule. Ramesh will coordinate timing that works across time zones to ensure convenient session times with Raj.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that schedules change. Please contact Ramesh at least 24 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling, and your purchased hours will remain available for future use.

Do unused hours expire?

Purchased tutoring hours are valid for 6 months from the date of purchase, giving you plenty of flexibility to use them as needed.

What's covered in the demo session?

The 40-minute demo includes a diagnostic assessment of your current level, a walkthrough of 2-3 sample questions showcasing Raj's teaching methodology, and a personalized strategy discussion for your Digital SAT preparation.

Can I combine different options?

Yes! Many students start with a demo session, then purchase hourly tutoring or join the 8-week intensive program. Contact Ramesh via WhatsApp to discuss the best combination for your needs.

For Indian Students: UPI, NEFT/IMPS, Bank Transfer
International: PayPal, Wise
